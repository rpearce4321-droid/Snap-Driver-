# Seeker Use-Case SOP

Last updated: 2026-02-08

## Purpose
Guide a Seeker through profile setup, linking, routes, badges, and work history.

## Profile setup
1. Complete all required profile fields.
2. Upload a profile photo and a vehicle photo.
3. Set availability with days of week and time blocks.
4. Confirm the portal shows the Seeker profile as complete.

## Linking
1. Complete the video confirmation step when applicable.
2. Approve the link when both parties agree.
3. Enable Working Together after the link is active.

## Routes
1. Browse routes and review schedule match information.
2. Use Interested to signal a route.
3. Keep route availability aligned with your profile schedule.

## Messages
1. Use direct messages only after linking is permitted.
2. Keep communications professional and on-topic.

## Badges and check-ins
1. Select required badges and any optional badges within tier limits.
2. Submit check-ins during the 48-hour window.
3. Use neutral for no impact and negative only when necessary.

## Work history
1. Review work history entries for accuracy.
2. Confirm work unit completions at cadence end.

## Trial and tiers
1. Trial allows browsing only and expires after 3 months.
2. A paid tier is required to link or connect.
